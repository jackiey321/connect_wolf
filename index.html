At Connect Wolf, we understand that moms can’t always be by their baby’s side.

It’s our priority to make you feel comfortable about your baby’s safety, regardless of what errands you’re running or if you’re just taking a day to relax. Being a parent is a big responsibility, so we want to make sure you’re equipped for the job! Precious Connect is comfortable for your baby and easy for you to use.

Attach Precious Connect Band

The soft silicone band can be attached to your baby’s ankle or wrist for maximum comfort. Bands are interchangeable, so you can choose one to match your baby's clothes!

Open Precious Connect App

Each Precious Connect band has a unique serial number for identification. Just connect it to the app to sync your baby’s information.

Monitor Your Baby

Check on how your baby is doing, whether you’re away at work or cooking dinner in the kitchen! Precious Connect lets you know your baby’s location, heart rate, and other stats to put you at ease.

Customize To Your Liking

Personalize your baby’s stats by picking what you want to know about your baby. Our app allows for easy customization--just toggle the features on and off to suit your preferences!
